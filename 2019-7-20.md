
## 1. menace

[ˈmɛnɪs]

- n 威胁；祸害
	> In my view you are a menace to the public.
	> 
	> 在我看来，你对公众是个祸害。

- v 危及；威胁
	> If you are menaced by someone, they threaten to harm you.
